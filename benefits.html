Benefits Package

 Printable Benefits Package 

Retirement

  • Monthly contribution of 3% of your salary in your 401(k), without the requirement of an employee contribution
  • Plus, an annual lump sum profit sharing of 10%, deposited at the end of each year.
  • Employees are immediately fully vested in the plan
  • Plan is administered by ShareBuilder (http://www.sharebuilder401k.com/)

Health Care

  • Full Medical, Dental, and Vision insurance coverage through Anthem BlueCross BlueShield PPO
  • Includes a Health Savings Account (HSA) for immediate family members.  HSA allows funds to be set aside and invested, to be used on future medical expenses, tax free!
  • Case Consulting contributes up to $4000 to employee HSAs
  • Short Term Disability, Long Term Disability, and Life Insurance provided

Time Off

  • 33 days of leave each year (PTO, Holidays and Training)

Professional Development and Certification

  • $3,500 per year towards professional development and certification
  • Includes certifications, screencasts, CBTs, conferences, books, etc.
  • 24 hours per year of PTO for professional development and certification

Additional Benefits

  • $1800 per calendar year towards purchase of employee computer/tablet or other technology related device
  • $2500 referral bonus for fully cleared candidates
  • Dependent care account (providing tax deferred savings for child care)
  • Verizon 4G Wifi Card

Case Consulting, Inc

Case Consulting, Inc. provides Information Technology Consulting services to the Federal Government.  We provide top-notch staff with experience in delivering superior system engineering expertise, project management support, and application design via Agile methodologies to.  Areas of proficiency include experience in running diverse software development teams to implement solutions across the Federal Government.  Located in Northern Virginia and started by Paul Farmer, the focus of Case Consulting is to provide exceptional services to a variety of clients across the Federal Government.

Case Consulting is committed to creating a close knit culture of technology professionals, focused on delivering superior expertise and support, and application development to customers in the Commercial and Federal Government markets.  Case Consulting recognizes that employees are its number one asset and therefore offers a salary and benefits package that reflect this.
